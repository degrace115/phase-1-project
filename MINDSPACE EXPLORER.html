Call of Duty is a first-person shooter video game franchise published by Activision. Starting out in 2003, it first focused on games set in World War II, but over time, the series has seen games set in modern times, the midst of the Cold War, futuristic worlds, and outer space.

How to Play

Here are the basics to get started with Call of Duty:

  • Choose your game mode: Campaign, Multiplayer, or Zombies.
  • Familiarize yourself with the controls. Movement is typically controlled with the WASD keys, and aiming and shooting with the mouse.
  • Complete objectives in the campaign or compete against other players in multiplayer modes.
  • Use a variety of weapons and equipment to gain the upper hand in battles.
  • Stay aware of your surroundings and work with your team to achieve victory.

Features

Call of Duty offers a wide range of features:

  • Multiple game modes including Campaign, Multiplayer, and Zombies.
  • Realistic graphics and immersive sound design.
  • Variety of maps and environments.
  • Extensive weapon customization options.
  • Regular updates and new content releases.

Game Controls

  • Move: WASD keys
  • Aim: Mouse
  • Shoot: Left mouse button
  • Reload: R key
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Crouch: C key
  • Switch Weapon: 1 and 2 keys
